About Studio Ghibli

    • Studio Ghibli is a renowned Japanese animation film studio founded in 1985 by the collaboration of directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, along with producer Toshio Suzuki. The studio is widely celebrated for its imaginative storytelling, richly detailed animation, and profound themes that captivate audiences of all ages around the world.
